Replay Board is used to replay your recorded game
or loaded file of record of game.
You can try another way to win changing screen to Free Board.
Use "Back" to return to Replay Board from Free Board.
-----
  "||<<" : Back to before the 1st move.
  "<<" : Back 10 moves.
  "<" : Back one move.
  ">>" : Forward 10 moves.
  "<" : Forward 1 move.
  ">>||" : Forward to the last move.
-----
"Close" : End Replay Board.
"Variation" : Change screen to Free Board.
"Setting" : Board options such as about sounds.
